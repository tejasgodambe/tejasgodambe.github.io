I am a third year MS student at the Information Retrieval and Extraction Laboratory at IIIT-H. My advisers are Professor Vasudeva Varma and Professor Manish Gupta. In 2013 I graduated from MIT Chennai with a Bachelor in Computer Science and Engineering. Prior to joining IIIT-H I worked at Hewlett Packard Imaging and Printing Group R&D in Bangalore, India.
